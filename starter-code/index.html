A wrapper div that contains all the HTML for that product. A div with a span, showing the product name. A div with a span, showing the cost of one unit. A div with one label and one input, where the user will indicate how many units they will buy. A div with a span, showing the total price for this product. This number should be the result of multiplying the amount of units multiplied by the price of one unit of that product. The default total price should be 0. A div with a delete button, to remove the product from the list.
